The PUSH

Push exercises build upper body strength by targeting muscles such as the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Consistent performance can increase the amount of weight you lift and build more muscle mass, resulting in greater overall strength and fitness.

The PULL

Pull exercises target and strengthen the muscles in your back, biceps, and forearms, helping to increase muscle mass and upper body strength. Pull exercises can help improve your overall athletic performance by building upper body strength

The LEGS

Leg-focused exercises like squats and lunges target and strengthen the muscles in your glutes, quads, hamstrings, and calves, leading to increased lower body strength and muscle mass over time. You will feel stronger and more capable overall.
